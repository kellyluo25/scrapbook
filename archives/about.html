about

origins: 2014 — now

i'm always thinking thinking about how the stories we consume and pay attention to (in culture and media) shape how we understand and interpret the world. this scrapbook holds all the stories i've paid attention to this year, and an archive of all stories past. to understand me as a person is to study the collective influences and references that sum to all of my fictional and imagined experiences.

xox.
